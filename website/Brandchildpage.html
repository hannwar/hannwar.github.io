Brand Assignment

 

To preface, I am not a big brands person. I don’t personally have opinions on brand names, as I don’t typically buy name brand items or clothing. That being said, the brand I have chosen to discuss is Champion. 

Champion (also recognized as Champion U.S.A.) is a clothing brand that sells casual wear clothing, footwear, and some accessories such as hats and bags. The brand is owned by Hanesbrands and is Hanes’s second largest brand. The company originally started back in 1919 by the Feinbloom Brothers. It was called the "Knickerbocker Knitting Company" and only produced sweatshirts and hoodies. The name was changed to Champion in 1989 and was bought out by Sara Lee Corporation. Why Champion? I chose Champion because I am a big fan of their hoodies and sweatshirts. A couple of my favorite hoodies are Champion hoodies. I can also appreciate the layout of the website. It’s nothing extravagant. When you first go to the site, you’re welcomed by complimenting colors of blue. One of the blues used is used in the logo. You also would see a large banner that is promoting a sale at the very top of the website, it’s one of the first things anyone would see.

 It’s clear that promotion and endorsement of products is their main focus of the website. I like how the nav bar is easily seen and the options are presented in an upper-case easy to read font. What Do I Plan To Use? I am a fan of the easy navigation used on the website. I don’t plan on using similar fonts, however. It’s clear from Champion’s large fonts that their goal is to promote products, which I will not be doing. Therefore, I’m going for a more artistic and catchy look. For my accent font I’m going to use the handwritten font Modern Love. I love the strokes on this font, it reminds me of paint strokes. For my body font I’m going to use the serif font Bell MT. For my logo I wanted to use something somewhat abstract with a little color.

 I decided on a multi-color soundwave image that I will insert text into. I’m thinking right in the middle of the soundwave. I like the simplicity of it and think it will add a nice theme for my website. I’m not sure on background color yet, but I’m thinking black or a dark grey. I’d like to accent my website with some bright colors to highlight my logo. Some blues and purples mostly with a splash of yellow.
